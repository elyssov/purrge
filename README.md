# PURRGE 🐱💥

**Kawaii roguelike-puzzle about a cat destroying an apartment while the owner is at work.**

*Purr + Purge = PURRGE*

---

## The Pitch

You are a cat. Your owner left for work. You have 9 hours. Everything in the apartment is made of voxels. Everything is destructible. Everything has a price tag.

Push the vase off the table. Watch it shatter. Chain reaction: the vase hits the lamp. The lamp falls on the laptop. The laptop slides off the desk. $4,200 in damages. The dog wakes up.

Your final score is a detailed repair bill. Share it with friends.

## Features

- **Full voxel destructibility** — every object shatters, crumbles, or snaps
- **Chain reactions** — Rube Goldberg machines made by a cat
- **Procedurally generated apartments** — different layout every run
- **Owner personality system** — gamer? artist? minimalist? affects item values
- **The Dog** — sleeps 70% of the time. Wakes up on loud noises. Gets in the way.
- **Cat customization** — color, ears, tail, size (affects gameplay)
- **Boredom meter** — stop destroying = die of boredom. Literally.
- **Nine lives** — falls, electrocution, heavy objects. You're a cat. You'll manage.
- **Street level** — get thrown out? 2D platformer escape. Find a new owner. New apartment. New cycle.
- **Final repair bill** — detailed, itemized, shareable. Viral potential.

## Built On

**[Prometheus Engine](https://github.com/elyssov/prometheus-engine)** — custom voxel engine with GPU raymarching, full destructibility, and procedural generation.

PURRGE is the showcase game for Prometheus Engine. Every subsystem of the engine is tested here before being used in larger projects.
